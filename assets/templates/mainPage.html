Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is personal soccer training, and how is it different from team training?

    Personal soccer training is one-on-one training with a coach who works specifically with the individual player to improve their skills and technique. Team training is more focused on developing teamwork and game tactics for a group of players.

  • Who is personal soccer training suitable for?

    Personal soccer training is suitable for players of all ages and skill levels who want to improve their game. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced player, there is always room for improvement and personalized training can help you reach your full potential.

  • What types of training do you offer?

    We offer a range of training programs, including technical training, speed and agility training, strength and conditioning, and game-specific training. We tailor our training to meet the individual needs and goals of each player.

  • How long are the training sessions?

    Training sessions typically last for one hour, although we can customize the length of the session to fit your schedule and availability.

  • Where do the training sessions take place?

    Training sessions can take place in a variety of locations, including indoor and outdoor facilities such as the KW FitHouse and RIM Park, depending on your preference and availability.

  • What qualifications do your coaches have?

    All of our coaches have extensive experience in coaching and playing soccer at a high level. They are also certified by reputable organizations such as Ontario Soccer and have completed background checks.

  • How often should I schedule a training session?

    The frequency of training sessions depends on your goals and availability. We recommend at least one session per week to see consistent improvement, but we can work with you to develop a schedule that meets your needs.

  • How do you track progress?

    We use a variety of methods to track progress, including video analysis, goal setting, and regular assessments. This allows us to identify areas of improvement and adjust our training programs accordingly.

  • What equipment do I need for training?

    We provide all necessary equipment, including balls, cones, and training aids. You should wear appropriate soccer attire and footwear.
